PVKingdoms iOS App

PVKingdoms (full title: PVKingdoms - Turn-based fantasy tactics) is a classic fantasy turn-based strategy game for iOS (iPhone, iPad, and Mac). It is a spiritual homage to early Heroes of Might and Magic (especially HoMM2) games, featuring heroes, castles, magic, and epic tactical battles.

Core Gameplay

  • Explore a hex-grid world with treasures, ruins, and neutral guardians.
  • Build and upgrade towns to boost your economy and unlock stronger units.
  • Recruit armies with multiple creature tiers and roles.
  • Lead heroes/commanders with skills and artifacts.
  • Fight tactical battles that emphasize terrain, positioning, and special abilities.
  • Capture towns, collect relics, and expand your realm across hand-crafted maps.

Key Features

  • Offline play (ideal for travel).
  • Fast turns with a clean, mobile-optimized UI.
  • Battery-friendly pacing.
  • Premium version ($3.99) offers full access, no ads, and enhanced offline features.

Details

  • Category: Strategy (Age rating: 9+ — infrequent/mild cartoon or fantasy violence).
  • App size: ~193–198 MB.
  • Requires iOS 15.0+ (also supports macOS).
  • Supports English + 32 other languages.
  • Actively updated with UI fixes, theme support, graphics improvements (AI-assisted sprites), layout adjustments for iPad landscape, and gameplay refinements (recent updates in 2026).

Overall

PVKingdoms is a thoughtful single-player fantasy strategy title ideal for fans of classic Heroes of Might and Magic who want a portable, turn-based experience with heroes, town-building, and tactical combat.

CTMayor iOS App

CTMayor (full title: CTMayor - City Simulation) is a city-building and transport simulation game for iOS (iPhone, iPad, and Mac). It combines three distinct simulation modes inspired by classic games like SimCity, Roman city-builders, and Transport Tycoon.

Core Modes

  • Ancient Rome Mode: Rule as a provincial governor in the Roman Empire. Build and manage prosperous cities, meet citizens' needs (food, housing, etc.), handle disasters, divine events, and threats.
  • Classic City Mode: Design and grow a modern city with zoning (residential, commercial, industrial), infrastructure, taxes, pollution management, and quality-of-life balancing. Inspired by early SimCity-style games.
  • Transport Company Mode: Build and operate a transport empire (trucks, buses, trains, planes, ships) from 1950–2050. Connect industries and towns, deliver cargo profitably, and aim for the highest performance rating.

Key Features

  • Real-time simulation with pause/fast-forward options, multiple speeds (including 5×/10×), and detailed management (budgets, infrastructure, vehicles, etc.).
  • Map overview, continuous building tools (long-press), vehicle refitting, and leaderboards.
  • Optimized for iPad, with iPhone portrait/landscape support and VoiceOver accessibility in some modes.
  • Free with ads and in-app purchases (e.g., yearly sponsorship ~$0.99 for premium features). A separate CTMayor Premium version exists.

Details

  • Category: Simulation (Age rating: 4+).
  • App size: ~203 MB.
  • Requires iOS 15.0+ (also supports macOS and visionOS).
  • Supports 33 languages.
  • Actively updated with UI improvements, new features (e.g., minimap vehicles, themes, accessibility), bug fixes, and gameplay refinements (recent updates in 2025–2026).

Overall

CTMayor is a versatile, single-player simulation bundle ideal for fans of classic city-builders and transport games who want variety in one app.

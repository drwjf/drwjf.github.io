Can I watch my IPTV on my phone?

Yes, you can use the Mapcasts App to listen IPTV for free. However, a subscription is required if you want to watch IPTV on your iPhone.

Mapcasts - AM/FM/TV Stations App (formerly known as RGRadio) brings over 40,000 AM/FM radio and TV stations from around the world to your fingertips.

You can also buffer the live streaming for offline playback when you have an unstable internet connection, for example, during commuting or camping.

Mapcasts is free from trackers and other bad stuff:

  • No advertisements
  • No tracking
  • No data collection
  • No phoning home
  • No annoying registration
  • No mandatory tutorials
  • No noisy email spam
  • No push notifications
  • No crapware

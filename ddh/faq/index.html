DDHeroes includes three strategy game modes for iOS platform:

  • Kingdoms Mode: Your aim is to rule the world by defeating all other heroes and capturing all of their castles. Inspired by Jon Van Caneghem's Heroes of Might and Magic II.
  • Dungeon Mode: Your goal is to travel to the 26th subterranean floor of the dungeon, retrieve the Amulet of Yendor and return with it to the surface. Based on Brian Walker's Brogue.
  • Cave Mode: You can explore a mysterious cave that is rumored to be filled with treasure and gold. Based on Willie Crowther's Colossal Cave Adventure.

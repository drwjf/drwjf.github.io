Introduction

Your aim is to rule the world by defeating all other heroes and capturing all of their castles.

The app previously offered four adventure modes: Kingdoms, Dungeon, Cave, and Escape.

Dungeon mode was removed due to licensing considerations, and Cave mode and Escape mode were removed based on user engagement. Then the app renamed from DDHeroes - Dungeon and Dragon to PVKingdoms.

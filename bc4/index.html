BC4000 is a single player, turn-based strategy game for iOS platform inspired by Sid Meier's Civilization and Steve Barcia's Master of Orion. Most graphics, music, map and text materials come from public domain licensed by Creative Commons License or General Public License. The core data structure design is impacted by the Freeciv project heavily.

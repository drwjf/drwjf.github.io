BC4000 includes three turn-based strategy game modes for iOS platform:

  • Civilization Mode You take on the role of the ruler of a civilization, attempt to build an empire in competition with other civilizations. The game begins in 4000 BC, before the Bronze Age, and can last through to AD 2100 with Space Age. Inspired by Sid Meier's Civilization.
  • Galaxy Mode You are cast as the immortal emperor who shapes the future of your race, as contact is made with the neighboring races. Your objective is simple: control a majority of the known galaxy and eliminate all who stand in the way. Inspired by Steve Barcia's Master of Orion
  • Battlefield Mode You command your units on a map of hexagons, trying to accomplish mission objectives ranging from defending important locations to simply destroying all enemy forces. Inspired by Blue Byte's Battle Isle.

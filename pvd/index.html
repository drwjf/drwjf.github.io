PVBigDeuce iOS App

PVBigDeuce (full title: PVBigDeuce - Big Two Learning) is an educational card game tutor app for iOS (iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision). It helps players learn and master Big Deuce (also known as Big Two, Chor Dai Di, Capsa, Pusoy Dos, etc.), a popular fast-paced shedding card game of Chinese origin.

Core Features

  • Play against three smart AI robots in 4-player games.
  • Built-in AI Tutor that provides guided hints, move suggestions, strategy explanations, and move comparison to help you improve.
  • Practice mode with hints or self-testing to verify and refine your strategy.
  • Focus on learning optimal play, hand management, and common tactics in this climbing/shedding game.

Key Details

  • Category: Education (Age rating: 9+).
  • Free with ads; PVBigDeuce Premium version available for an ad-free experience.
  • Very small app size: ~10.1 MB.
  • Requires iOS 15.0+ (also supports macOS and visionOS).
  • Supports English + 32 other languages.
  • Regularly updated with UI refinements, theme customization, portrait mode for iPhone, stability improvements, and iOS compatibility (latest updates in 2025–2026).

Overall

PVBigDeuce is a focused learning tool ideal for beginners and intermediate players wanting to improve at Big Two/Big Deuce against competent AI with tutor support.

TourTool iOS App

TourTool - Interpreter & Guide is a travel utility app focused on real-time translation, transcription, and sightseeing assistance for iOS (iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision). It functions as a portable interpreter tool ideal for travelers, international conversations, and exploration.

Core Features

  • Real-time Chat Interpreter/Translator: Recognizes speech from you or your conversation partner, displays text, translates it accurately, and speaks it back in the target language.
  • Speech Transcriber: Instantly shows spoken words as text (useful for listening to speeches or conversations).
  • Offline English Dictionary: Frequency-sorted word list for quick reference.
  • Sightseeing Guide: Uses location access to suggest nearby famous landmarks (works offline for basic functionality even without network).

Key Details

  • Supports a large number of languages (over 40, including Mandarin, Cantonese, Shanghainese, major European/Asian languages, Arabic, Hindi, etc.).
  • Category: Travel (Age rating: 4+).
  • Free (likely with ads, consistent with the developer’s other apps).
  • App size: ~131.2 MB.
  • Requires iOS 12.0+ (also supports macOS and visionOS).
  • Supports many UI languages.

Overall

TourTool is a focused, all-in-one travel language tool that emphasizes speech translation, transcription, and basic sightseeing help.

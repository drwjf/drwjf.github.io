Subscription Update Notice

Some users may have noticed that the legacy yearly subscription price has changed to $364.99/year. This price is not intended to be an actual active subscription offering.

Because there are still existing subscribers associated with the old yearly subscription product, the product cannot be fully deleted from App Store Connect. Instead, the price was intentionally increased to prevent new subscriptions and renewals on the discontinued yearly plan.

The annual subscription model was discontinued because it became difficult to confidently guarantee uninterrupted long-term service and support over a full year period. A few months ago, server-side issues caused several days of service interruption, and that experience led to reconsideration of long-term prepaid subscriptions.

The current subscription option is a weekly plan priced at $0.69/week. The goal is to keep pricing flexible and fair so users can stop subscribing at any time if the service no longer meets expectations or if technical issues arise, instead of being locked into a long annual commitment.

The app does not require user accounts, does not collect subscriber email addresses, and does not track personal user information. Because of this privacy-focused design, there is no direct way to email all subscribers about subscription changes. Updates and discussions have been posted in the Discord community, though it is understood that these announcements may not reach every subscriber.

Thank you to all long-time users and supporters for your understanding and continued support.

Additionally, a $30/month support subscription remains available for users who would like to provide additional financial support for ongoing costs, maintenance, and future development. This higher-priced option is entirely optional and is not required to access the normal service experience.

Tubecasts Air iOS App

Tubecasts Air (full title: Tubecasts Air - Audio Only Player) is a YouTube audio streaming app for iOS (iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision). It is the successor to the original Tubecasts app (which has been retired) and focuses on playing YouTube videos and playlists as background audio.

Core Features

  • Play YouTube playlists and videos in the background with audio-only mode (saves battery and prevents screen drain).
  • Create and manage custom playlists/folders.
  • Support for background playback, CarPlay, and continued listening with the screen locked.
  • Offline buffering options (Premium), playlist import/export, and multi-audio track selection.
  • Lightweight and focused interface with customizable themes.
  • Strong emphasis on privacy: No ads, no tracking, no data collection, no registration, and no push notifications.

Key Details

  • Category: Music (Age rating: 18+ due to YouTube content access).
  • Free and in-app purchases (sponsorship options ranging from low weekly to higher monthly subscriptions).
  • App size: ~34.2 MB.
  • Requires iOS 15.0+ (also supports macOS and visionOS).
  • Supports English + 32 other languages.

Overall

Tubecasts Air is a straightforward, no-frills tool for turning YouTube content into background audio streams.

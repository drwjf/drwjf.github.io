Can I play YouTube in background on iPhone?

Yes. You can use Tubecasts Air App to play YouTube videos in the background.

With Tubecasts Air App, you can enjoy playing your favorite YouTube playlists and videos in the background and audio-only mode. This feature significantly extends your device's screen and battery life.

You can also buffer the audio files for offline playback when you have an unstable internet connection, for example, during commuting or camping.

Tubecasts Air Blog

The original "Tubecasts" app has been retired and replaced with the "Tubecasts Air" app. While no new updates for "Tubecasts" are available, users can still reinstall and continue using it from their App Store account purchase history.

We strongly recommend trying the "Tubecasts Air" app. It is free to install and is continuously updated to meet the latest development requirements.

Initial at 2023

With Tubecasts Air, you can enjoy playing your favorite YouTube playlists and videos in the background and audio-only mode. This feature significantly extends your device's screen and battery life.

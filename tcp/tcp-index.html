Tubecasts - Audio only player for YouTube

The Tubecasts app supports playing your favorite YouTube playlists/videos in AUDIO ONLY energy efficiency mode. The lifetime of your device's screen and internet data usage could be saved drastically.

You can save YouTube video streams to your album to play offline without worrying about cell data plan and signal quality on the road.

You can try the basic playback feature by App Clip - even before installing the full app.

The Tubecast app is inspired by the library YouTube-Player-iOS-Helper provided by YouTube.

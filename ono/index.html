Order & Occupy Blog

Initial at 2023

  • Order & Occupy is set in a future governed by superpowers engaged in cutthroat competition for the galaxy's resources. The game centers on two specific factions vying for control of the recently discovered planet Icarus. This planet holds an immense reservoir of galaxite, a rare and valuable mineral essential for teleportation.
  • Players must ascend the corporate hierarchy by mining the planet and locating a missing security specialist. Throughout the game, players will uncover enigmatic alien technologies, including a device capable of duplicating objects, and encounter a new extraterrestrial threat to humanity.
  • The game's logic and user interaction concept draw inspiration from the classic Real-Time Strategy game Command & Conquer. The game has utilized graphics, music, maps, and text materials available in the public domain, all licensed under the BSD open-source license or Creative Commons License.

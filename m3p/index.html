M3Player iOS App

M3Player (full title: M3Player - M3U & M3U8 Player) is a lightweight playlist and streaming player for iOS (iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision). It specializes in handling M3U and M3U8 playlist files, with strong support for background playback.

Core Features

  • Import and play M3U/M3U8 playlists from URLs or local files.
  • Organize streams and links into folders for easy management.
  • Audio-only mode — saving battery and screen.
  • Background playback, sleep timer.
  • Clean, modern interface focused on simplicity and efficiency.
  • Strong emphasis on privacy: No ads, no tracking, no data collection, no registration, and no push notifications.

Note: The app does not provide content — users must supply their own playlists/streams.

Key Details

  • Category: Entertainment (Age rating: 18+).
  • Requires iOS 15.0+ (also supports macOS and visionOS).
  • Supports English + 32 other languages.

Overall

M3Player is a focused, no-frills utility for playlist enthusiasts, internet radio listeners, and users who want lightweight audio streaming (especially audio from video sources) on iOS.

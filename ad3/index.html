AD3000 iOS Game

AD3000 (also called AD3000 - Orion Master) is a turn-based sci-fi 4X strategy game with an additional open-world Galaxy RPG / space adventure mode. It is available on the iOS App Store for iPhone and iPad (requires iOS 15.0+).

Core Features

  • 4X Strategy Mode (inspired by classics like Master of Orion): Lead one of 10 unique races to dominate a galaxy. Expand via exploration, colonization, diplomacy, trade, conquest, resource management, technology research, and custom ship design. Features procedural galaxies, AI opponents, and victory conditions (including a galactic council).
  • Galaxy RPG / Space Adventure Mode: Play as a free captain in an open universe. Trade goods across planets, upgrade your ship and fleet, battle pirates, mine asteroids, take missions, and explore.

Key Details

  • Free version includes ads and some limitations. A Premium version ($3.99) offers an ad-free experience, full access, offline play, and extra features.
  • Optimized especially for iPad (full-screen planet views, etc.), with support for iPhone (including recent portrait/landscape options).
  • Size: ~150 MB (free) / larger for Premium.
  • Age rating: 9+ (infrequent/mild violence, weapons).
  • Actively updated with UI improvements, new modes (e.g., Alien Empire), localization, performance fixes, and RPG enhancements.

Overall

It's a deep, single-player space strategy/RPG hybrid well-suited for fans of classic 4X games who want something playable on iOS devices.

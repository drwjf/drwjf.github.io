CCAdventure iOS App

CCAdvent (full title: CCAdvent - Geography learning or CCAdvent - Civilization & Culture Adventures) is an educational exploration app for iOS (iPhone, iPad, and Mac), . It offers a virtual world-travel experience focused on learning geography, history, and world cultures.

Core Features

  • Virtually travel around the world to visit famous heritage sites, landmarks, and cultural locations.
  • View high-quality photos and access detailed information about each place (sourced from Wikipedia under CC BY-SA 3.0 license).
  • Combines geography learning with historical and cultural context in an engaging, adventure-style format.
  • Designed for casual, self-paced exploration rather than structured gameplay or strategy.

Key Details

  • Category: Education (Age rating: 4+).
  • Free app (previously included ads; later updates removed them).
  • App size: ~258 MB.
  • Requires iOS 12.0+ (also supports iPadOS and macOS).
  • Supports many languages (English + 32 others).
  • Regularly updated with bug fixes, iOS compatibility improvements, and stability enhancements (most recent updates in 2025).

Overall

CCAdvent is a peaceful, educational virtual travel app focused on geography, history, and culture. It’s ideal for light, informative exploration on iOS devices.

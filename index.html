Strategy Games

BC4000 - Civilization Model

Take the role of tribal leader in 4000 B.C., move units on the map, build or improve new cities and units, and choose technologies to research to enable new building skills turn by turn. Inspired by Sid Meier's Civilization. Most assets are licensed from Freeciv.

DDHeroes - Dragon Dungeon Hero

  • Rule the world by commanding your heroes battling other heroes armies, gaining experience and learning new skills, collecting resources and artifacts, capturing mines and castles and building improvements to existing castles in order to produce and hire more powerful creatures. Inspired by Jon Van Caneghem's Heroes of Might and Magic II.
  • Travel to the 26th subterranean floor of the dungeon, retrieve the Amulet of Yendor and return with it to the surface. Most assets are licensed from Brian Walker's Brogue.
  • Explores a cave system rumored to be filled with treasure and gold. Most assets are licensed from Willie Crowther's Colossal Cave Adventure.

CTMayor - City Simulation

  • Create your own city from the ground up. Or become the master of existing cities to solve horrible planning issues or rescue citizens from a natural disaster. Most assets are licensed from Will Wright's SimCity.
  • Assume the role of a provincial governor to build thriving cities across the Roman Empire, in which they must ensure their citizens have their needs met, and deal with various disasters, angry gods and hostile enemies. Inspired by Sierra Studios' Caesar III. Most assets are licensed from dalerank's Caesaria and Bianca van Schaik's Julius.
  • player acts as an entrepreneur in control of a transport company, and can compete against rival companies to make as much profit as possible by transporting passengers and various goods by road, rail, sea and air. Inspired by Chris Sawyer's Transport Tycoon Deluxe. Most assets are licensed from Hansjörg Malthane's Sumutrans, Ludvig Strigeus's OpenTTD, Richard Wheeler's OpenGFX2, and OpenSFX.

AD3000 - Orion Master

  • Lead one of ten races to dominate the galaxy through a combination of diplomacy and conquest while developing technology, exploring and colonizing star systems. Inspired by the Steve Barcia's Master of Orion.
  • Explore a vast 2D galaxy—trade, fight, mine, or take missions as you travel through star systems. Align with factions, build your fleet, and forge your own path. Inspired by Escape Velocity, Endless Sky and Naev.
  • Most assets are licensed from Remnants of the Precursors and Endless Sky.

BTFields - World War

  • Command a number of units on a map of hexagons, trying to accomplish mission objectives ranging from defending important locations to simply destroying all enemy forces. Inspired by Blue Byte's Battle Isle. Most assets are licensed from Crimson Fields.
  • A turn based strategy mode simulates conflict during World War II. Entrenchment, rugged defense, defensive fire, surprise contacts, surrender, unit supply, weather influence, reinforcements and other implementations contribute to the tactical and strategic depth. Inspired by Panzer General. Most assets are licensed from lGeneral.
  • Build your forces to defend and control the newly discovered planet Icarus, with its astounding mineral wealth and mysterious alien artifacts. Most assets are licensed from Warfare Incorporated.

My iPad & iPhone Strategy Game Apps

BC4000 - Civilization Model

Take the role of tribal leader in 4000 B.C., move units on the map, build or improve new cities and units, and choose technologies to research to enable new building skills turn by turn. Inspired by the Sid Meier's Civilization. P.S. The game includes `galaxy mode` also. Player can explore and colonize star systems from A.D. 3000. Inspired by the Steve Barcia's Master of Orion.

DDHeroes - Dragon Dungeon Hero

Rule the world by commanding your heroes battling other heroes armies, gaining experience and learning new skills, collecting resources and artifacts, capturing mines and castles and building improvements to existing castles in order to produce and hire more powerful creatures. Inspired by the Jon Van Caneghem's Heroes of Might and Magic II.

CTMayor - City Simulation

Create your own dream city from the ground up. Or become the master of existing cities to solve horrible planning issues or rescue citizens from a natural disaster. Inspired by the Will Wright's SimCity.

BF1 - Battle Fields

Commands a number of units on a map of hexagons, trying to accomplish mission objectives ranging from defending important locations to simply destroying all enemy forces. Damaged units may be repaired or replaced by new recruits, while victorious squadrons will improve their abilities so they will be even more difficult to beat next time. Inspired by the Blue Byte's Battle Isle.

PVBridge - Contract bridge

Three AI robots play the contract bridge game with you anytime and anywhere. Nature bidding system rules and Alpha-Beta Pruning algorithm.

PVBigDeuce - Big two learning

AI tutor will tell you how to play big deuce. And three AI robots play with you anytime and anywhere. Big Deuce Wiki here.

PVMahjong - Mahjong learning

AI tutor will tell you how to play mahjong. And three AI robots play with you anytime and anywhere. Game follows Babcock rules.

PVChess - Chess learning

A world-champion level chess tutor and six sparring partners. Beginners can follow hint to learn how to play as an expert step by step.

PVXiangqi - Chinese chess learning

A world-champion level chinese chess tutor and six sparring partners. Beginners can follow hint to learn how to play as an expert step by step.

PVGo - Weiqi Go Baduk learning

A Baduk (weiqi) (igo) tutor and six sparring partners. Beginners can follow hint to learn how to play as an expert step by step.

PVRenju - Renju Gomoku learning

A Renju (Five in a row) (Gomoku) tutor and six sparring partners. Beginners can follow hint to learn how to play as an expert step by step.
